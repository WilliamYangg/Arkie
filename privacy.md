# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** [26 May 2026]

This Privacy Policy describes how Arkie ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Arkie iOS app and related services (the "Service").

## Information we collect

**Information you provide directly.** When you use Arkie, you may create entries, capture photos, and write notes. This content is stored on your device and, where you opt in, synced to our servers.

**Account information.** If you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication identifiers.

**Connected accounts.** If you connect a third-party account (such as TikTok, Instagram, or other social platforms) to publish content, we receive limited information from that platform necessary to publish on your behalf, including access tokens and your account identifier. We do not receive your password.

**Usage information.** We collect basic information about how you use the Service, such as feature usage and crash reports, to improve the app.

## How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the Service
- Publish content to connected third-party platforms when you request it
- Communicate with you about the Service
- Improve and develop new features
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

## How we share information

We do not sell your personal information.

We share information only in the following cases:

- **With third-party platforms you connect**, solely to publish content you have authorized us to publish
- **With service providers** who help us operate the Service (such as hosting and analytics providers), under confidentiality obligations
- **When required by law** or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Arkie, our users, or others

## Third-party platforms

When you connect a third-party account such as TikTok, that platform's own privacy policy governs how it handles your data. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any platform you connect.

## Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information, and to withdraw consent for certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children

Arkie is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have, please contact us and we will delete it.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version here and update the effective date above. Material changes will be communicated through the Service.
